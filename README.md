# TransitOps 🚛

TransitOps is a smart fleet and transport operations platform built by **Team Overdrive**.

We built it to bring everything involved in fleet operations — vehicles, drivers, trips, maintenance, compliance, expenses and customer tracking — into one place, while also helping users make smarter operational decisions.

## What it does

- **Smart Assign** — Recommends the best vehicle and driver combinations for a trip based on availability, capacity, compliance, maintenance risk and driver safety.

- **Smart Maintenance** — Analyzes vehicle data and gives each vehicle an explainable maintenance risk score.

- **Compliance Autopilot** — Tracks vehicle documents and driver licences, sends expiry warnings and blocks non-compliant resources from being dispatched.

- **Dynamic Pricing** — Estimates trip prices based on distance, fuel cost, vehicle efficiency, operational costs and demand.

- **Expense Anomaly Detection** — Flags unusual expenses by comparing them against historical data for review.

- **Fleet Tracking** — Gives logistics clients a secure Tracking ID to check their shipment progress without exposing internal company data.

- **Tamper-Evident Audit Ledger** — Uses SHA-256 hash chaining to make changes to operational history detectable.

- **Data Dashboard & Reports** — Provides role-specific analytics for revenue, expenses, fleet utilization, maintenance, safety and compliance, with CSV/PDF export options.

## Role-Based Access

TransitOps provides separate access for:

- Fleet Manager
- Dispatcher
- Safety Officer
- Financial Analyst
- System Administrator
- Logistics Client

Each role can only view or manage the information relevant to them.
